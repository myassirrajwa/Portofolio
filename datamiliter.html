DATA MILITARY

This website is developed using HTML, CSS, and PHP, and is specifically designed to manage military data in order to assist security . officers in verifying the identity and purpose of individuals who wish to visit their military family members The system allows every visit to be recorded in an organized and systematic manner, making it easier for security personnel to detect whether someone entering the military area has proper authorization. In addition, this platform plays an important role in maintaining the safety of military housing by providing alerts or warnings if an unknown or suspicious person attempts to access the military premises without clear permission.
