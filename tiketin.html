TIKETINAJA

TiketinAja is a web-based ticketing platform designed to support the organization and accessibility of local events in a more efficient and structured manner. Built with Laravel 11, Livewire, and integrated QR Code technology, the platform offers an end-to-end solution for both event organizers and attendees. Key features such as event management, e-ticket system, and admin panel provide ease of use and enhance the overall user experience. With a clean and responsive user interface, TiketinAja aims to become a reliable medium that promotes the growth of creative communities and local cultural events.
