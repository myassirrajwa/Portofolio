About US

At SMK IT Assyifa, I began my journey in web development during 10th grade by learning and building websites using Laravel, a popular and powerful PHP framework. This gave me a strong foundation in backend development, database management, and the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture. I have created several web-based projects, including dynamic websites with user login systems, admin dashboards, and ticket booking platforms. Now, as an 11th-grade student, I’m continuing to grow my skills by building this portfolio website to showcase my projects, achievements, and learning progress. In addition to web development, I have also started learning Flutter, a modern mobile app development framework built by Google. Flutter uses the Dart programming language and allows me to develop cross-platform applications for both Android and iOS from a single codebase. Besides programming, I’m also passionate about UI/UX design. To strengthen my skills in this area, I have taken various online courses and design training using Figma. As a result, I have earned 10 Figma certificates, covering topics such as UI/UX fundamentals, responsive design, interactive prototyping, and collaborative design workflows. These accomplishments reflect my dedication to continuous learning across both development and design fields, and my commitment to creating digital products that are not only functional but also visually engaging.
